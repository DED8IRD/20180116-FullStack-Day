Ralph Reed

Ralph Reed (born 1943) is an English painter and drawer associated with the poetic landscape movement. Reed currently resides and exhibits in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Born in Sheffield England, Ralph Reed studied at Sheffield College of Art, the Royal Academy in London and at Indiana University. He has had over 14 solo shows in Europe and America. Reed is known for The Sky Diary, a daily drawing of the sky starting in September 1973 and still active. One of Reed's greatest influences includes Joseph Turner, the romantic painter from England. "In 1973, I began The Sky Diary, a coloured drawing of the sky everyday. Forty-plus years of daily skies also gives me a pictorial and metaphorical starting point for my work. The paintings are of the landscape. The underlying subject matter is of memory, loss, and the uniting of poetry and painting."
